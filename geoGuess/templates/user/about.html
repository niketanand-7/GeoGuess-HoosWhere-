About HoosWhere?

HoosWhere? is a geoguesser game designed specifically for the UVA community. It challenges students to put their campus knowledge to the test by identifying different locations around the University of Virginia based on images.

How to Play

  1. Start the game by logging in with your UVA student credentials.
  2. Once logged in, click on Daily Challenge and you will be presented multiple challenges. You can play past challenges or the daily challenge.
  3. Your task is to guess the location by placing a pin on the provided map.
  4. Submit your guess to see how close you were to the actual location by clicking on submit challenge.
  5. Earn points based on how accurate your guess was and climb up the leaderboard!

We also encourage you to create your own challenges, and if they are approved, they will appear as a Daily Challenge for other users in the future!

Whether you're a new student or a seasoned Hoo, this game is a fun way to explore the campus, learn about new spots, and see how well you really know UVA. Get guessing and go Hoos!
